Macaroni Salad Recipe

Homepage

Description

A classic macaroni salad that has all the nutrients you need!

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Condiments
  • Sugar and Vinegar
  • Seasonings
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Cook macaroni in salted water
  2. Mix the mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, and seasonings together
  3. Stir in the macaroni and remaining ingredients. Chill in the refrigerator
